HOW IT WORKS

Select your package ,
Create an account on Ultimateaccess,fill out your details,click on register button you would be sent a link to your Email and a code to your phone number fill that out and you are automatically registered.

DONATION

When a package is selected, you wait for the system to find a member ready to receive help. Once this is done, your countdown to complete payment begins, once paid click on I have paid button. For security purposes pay only to the account details you see on your dashboard and kindly call the receiver before and after payment for confirmation. Upload your POP.
Once your position has been confirmed activated, it will be queued on the list of the position that will be available for new members to receive help. It usually takes 72hrs – 7days.

RE-DONATE

This is automatic once you have paid out your first ph the system would merge u to pay your second ph which is 50% of your expected gh,when payments are completed you get help. Don’t forget your GH is someone’s PH. Re-donation is copulsory to ensure the system is safe and healthy.
